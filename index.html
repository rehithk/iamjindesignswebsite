Our Process

We follow a thoughtful, personalized design journey to bring your dream space to life.

1. Understanding You

We begin by getting to know you—your needs, preferences, lifestyle, and vision for the space. We also study your site, floor plans, budget, and timeline to build the right foundation.

2. Planning the Layout

Based on our discussions, we create floor plans, furniture layouts, and structural suggestions. We also share a clear design scope and fee for approval.

3. Design Concept

We present mood boards, design ideas, and reference images to help you visualize the space. Your feedback helps us refine the concept and estimate the budget.

4. Design Details

After finalizing the concept, we create technical drawings and a detailed material list covering furniture, electricals, plumbing, and more.

5. Execution & Supervision

Our team supervises the site to ensure timely progress, quality work, and smooth coordination. You stay updated every step of the way.

6. Final Touch & Handover

We add the final styling, inspect every detail, and hand over a beautiful, finished space—just as you imagined, or even better.
